## 1. Purpose
Define operational steps to process nonconformities and execute effective CAPA.

## 2. Scope
Applies to process, documentation, supplier, and product nonconformities in QMS scope.

## 3. Procedure
1. Log nonconformity with objective evidence and containment action.
2. Determine if CAPA is required (severity, recurrence, systemic risk).
3. Perform root-cause analysis using approved method.
4. Define corrective and preventive actions with owners and due dates.
5. Verify implementation completion and effectiveness.
6. Close CAPA only with documented effectiveness evidence.
7. Feed outcomes into management review and metrics.

## 4. Required Records
- Nonconformity record
- CAPA plan with root cause
- Effectiveness check evidence
