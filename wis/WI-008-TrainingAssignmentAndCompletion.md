## 1. Purpose
Define how QMS training obligations are assigned, completed, evidenced, and reviewed.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all required SOP/WI training triggered by role mapping or controlled document changes.

## 3. Procedure
1. Update role-to-document requirements in `matrices/training_matrix.yml`.
2. Create or refresh required training tasks after controlled document release.
3. Verify trainee completion with checklist and signed attestation where required.
4. Update training log with revision-level completion status.
5. Escalate overdue items to line manager and QA Lead.
6. Review closure rate and overdue trends during management review.

## 4. Required Records
- Training issue/task records
- Updated training log entries
- Escalation and closure evidence
