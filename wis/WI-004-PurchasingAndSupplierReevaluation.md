## 1. Purpose
Define execution steps for supplier qualification, purchase authorization, and periodic re-evaluation.

## 2. Scope
Applies to suppliers that can impact product quality, safety, regulatory compliance, or data integrity.

## 3. Procedure
1. Classify supplier criticality using quality and regulatory impact criteria.
2. Complete initial vetting checklist and collect objective evidence.
3. Approve supplier before first purchase; record in approved supplier list.
4. Define quality requirements in purchase/order terms.
5. Log receipt verification and any supplier-related nonconformity.
6. Re-evaluate suppliers on planned cadence or after significant incidents.
7. Escalate failing supplier performance to CAPA or disqualification decision.

## 4. Required Records
- Supplier vetting package
- Approved supplier list entry
- Re-evaluation decision record
