## 1. Purpose
Define standard triage flow for feedback, complaints, post-market signals, and escalation decisions.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all incoming product quality or safety signals from users, customers, and monitoring channels.

## 3. Procedure
1. Register incoming signal with source, date, and summary.
2. Classify signal type (feedback, complaint, incident candidate, trend item).
3. Perform initial severity and reportability screening.
4. Assign investigation owner and target completion date.
5. Determine linkage to risk file updates, CAPA, and regulatory reporting.
6. Record final disposition and communication to reporter where required.
7. Include item in PMS trend analysis.

## 4. Required Records
- Intake/triage record
- Investigation summary and disposition
- Linkage to CAPA/risk/reporting records
