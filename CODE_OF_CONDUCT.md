# Code of Conduct

Participate in this project in a respectful, professional, and constructive way.

Expected behavior:

- Focus on technical substance and regulatory clarity.
- Assume good intent and discuss disagreements directly.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and personal attacks.
- Respect confidentiality boundaries when discussing customer or company-specific deployments.

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that do not meet this standard.

Report conduct concerns to [aliaksei@dearauditor.ch](mailto:aliaksei@dearauditor.ch).
